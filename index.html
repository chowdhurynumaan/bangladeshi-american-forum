Our Mission

The Bangladeshi American Forum (BAF) is a non-profit organization dedicated to fostering cultural heritage, community engagement, and civic participation among Bangladeshi Americans in Michigan.

We organize various social, cultural, and educational programs throughout the year to serve our members and promote a deeper understanding of our culture.
